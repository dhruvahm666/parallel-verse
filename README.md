## 📌 What is Parallel Universe?

Parallel Universe is an AI-powered life simulation platform that generates **personalized alternate life timelines** based on your own dreams, ambitions, opportunities, and constraints.

Unlike generic career simulators, Parallel Universe doesn't rely on preset paths.  
You enter your own **"What If?" question** — the AI builds a realistic, deeply personalized universe around it.

> *"What if I became the first billionaire of my bloodline?"*  
> *"What if I built a company that transformed my village?"*  
> *"What if I wrote a Nobel Prize-winning book?"*

---

## ✨ Features

| Feature | Description |
|---|---|
| 🎯 Dream-Driven AI Analysis | Enter any dream or "What If?" — the AI tailors everything to your unique goals |
| 🌌 3 Parallel Universes | Get three distinct future paths, each with milestones, challenges, and outcomes |
| 📈 Interactive Timelines | Explore life progression from your current age to 80, milestone by milestone |
| 🧠 AI-Powered Insights | Uncover your core motivations, hidden patterns, biggest opportunities, and first steps |
| 🔮 Future Self Conversations | Chat with AI-generated versions of your future self from each parallel universe |
| 💰 Wealth & Impact Projections | See realistic financial growth and real-world impact estimates per path |
| 🌟 Real-World Inspiration | Each timeline surfaces similar achievers who walked a comparable path |

## 🚀 How It Works

> **Step 1** — Sign in with Google or Email  
> **Step 2** — Build your profile — age, background, current situation  
> **Step 3** — Enter your "What If?" dream, as specific or as bold as you want  
> **Step 4** — The AI generates 3 parallel universes tailored to your goals  
> **Step 5** — Explore each timeline — milestones, challenges, skills, finances  
> **Step 6** — Converse with your future self and uncover your recommended first actions

## 🎨 Design Philosophy

Parallel Universe is built to feel **magical, personal, and honest**.

Every timeline balances ambition with realism — showing not just the dream,  
but the exact effort, trade-offs, and obstacles required to reach it.

No generic advice. No preset careers. Just your universe.
